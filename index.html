He's lived in various cities and towns near the New York state capital of Albany. Most recently he lived and worked in the beautiful little city of Troy, which is rich in New England history and home to Uncle Sam.

He has two younger siblings, a brother and a sister. Both are in their twenties and are weird and wonderful people, trying to make sense out of the lives they've been given, just like you and me. You would like them.

Well, he's a produce man by trade, having worked his whole adult life buying and selling fruits and vegetables in retail and wholesale capacities. Need to know what to do with a kohlrabi? He's your dude.

A man of many interests, he also enjoys playing and listening to a wide variety of musical styles, is an avid reader, and is an enthusiastic (but not great,) player of low-division recreational co-ed soccer.
